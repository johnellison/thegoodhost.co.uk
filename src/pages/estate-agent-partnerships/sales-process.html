Sales agent partnership process

We leverage cutting-edge technology and operational expertise to simplify and ensure quality in all that we do.

Setup your agent portal

We'll provide you access to a private portal that you can use to provide short-term rental valuations for your vendors in real-time. You can check the progress of leads and analyze the performance of your team across locations.

Prepare, photograph & list on Airbnb, Booking.com, etc.

Once one of your vendors has signed up, we will professionally stage, dress and photograph for listing on platforms like Airbnb, Booking.com and HomeAway.

Welcome guests & maximize revenue

We manage the entire process of hosting guests who pay on a nightly basis while your vendor's property is on the market. This means your vendor earns during the entire sales process. You don't have to do anything.

Book a viewing

You can book viewings seamlessly around short-term guests to ensure that the sales process continues as smoothly as possible.

Go under contract

Once your property goes under contract, we will agree a date to discontinue short-term rental bookings to proceed to a swift exchange and completion after conveyancing.

Exchange & complete

During exchange we'll remove the property from all short-term rental sites and remove all furnishings (unless the buyer wishes to acquire them). At completion your vendor will be satisfied with the increased sale price and revenue they received while the property was on the market.
