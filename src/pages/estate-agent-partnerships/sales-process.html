Sales partnership process

Our partnership process for sales agents is simple

We leverage cutting-edge technology and operational expertise to simplify and ensure quality in all that we do.

Setup your portal with us

We'll provide you access to a private portal that you can use to provide short-term rental valuations for your customers in real-time.

Prepare property & photograph

Once your customer has agreed to list the property with you, we will professionally stage, dress and photograph for listing on platforms like Airbnb, ing.com and HomeAway.

Welcome guests & maximize revenue

You can welcome short-term guests who pay on a nightly basis while your property is on the market, in between viewings.

Book a viewing

You can book viewings seamlessly around short-term guests to ensure that the sales process continues as smoothly as possible.

Go under contract

Once your property goes under contract, we will agree a date to discontinue short-term rental bookings to proceed to a swift exchange and completion after conveyancing.

Exchange & complete

During exchange we'll remove the property from all short-term rental sites and remove all furnishings (unless the buyer wishes to acquire them in situ). At completion your vendor will be satisfied with the increased sale price and revenue they received while the property was on the market.
